Our Story

An ancestral home,
open to the world

"Not a hotel. A home that happens to have a few empty rooms."

Venad — the ancient Kerala kingdom from which our name comes — once stretched across these very backwaters. The home has stood here since 1924, built from laterite stone and century-old teak by Krishnan Varma, our great-grandfather.

We opened our doors to guests in 2019. Not because we needed to, but because this place was too good to keep to ourselves. There is no reception desk, no room service, no itinerary handed to you at check-in. Just us, the family, and everything Kerala has to offer outside your window.
